# Nirmal Memorial Site

A living tribute website for Dr. Nirmal Singh Ahluwalia (27.09.1939 – 28.06.2026), where family and friends read his story, browse photos of his life, and leave their own memories.

## Language

**Memory**:
A guest's tribute submission: their name, relationship to Nirmal, a message, and optionally a personal photo of themselves (or with him). Shown on the Memories wall.
_Avoid_: Guestbook entry, tribute, comment

**Gallery**:
The shared collection of photos of Nirmal's life — seeded by the admin from family archives, and grown by guests. Distinct from a Memory's personal photo: everything in the Gallery is a photo of Nirmal.
_Avoid_: Photo wall, album, group photos

**Personal photo**:
An optional photo a guest attaches to their own Memory, of themselves or themselves with Nirmal. Lives only inside that Memory's card — never added to the Gallery.
_Avoid_: Profile photo, avatar

**Relationship**:
A short label a guest supplies describing how they knew Nirmal (e.g. "Granddaughter", "Friend from Mombasa"), shown alongside their name on a Memory.

**Guest password**:
The site-wide password that unlocks the public site (Home, Gallery, Memories) for a long-lived session (~30–90 days). Distinct from the Admin password.
_Avoid_: Site password

**Admin password**:
The second password accepted at the Entry gate. Unlocks the admin view (edit/delete Memories, manage Gallery photos) instead of the public site, within the same session.

**Entry gate**:
The single password screen every visitor sees before anything else loads. Routes to either the public site or the admin view depending on which password was entered.
_Avoid_: Login screen, admin login, admin route
